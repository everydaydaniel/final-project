Block Manager

What is Block Manager?

Block manager is a web service that allows users to create accounts and keep track of their cryptocurrency positions. Users can track prices across all exchanges combining all their portfolios into one.

Why use Block Manager?

In the ever growing world of cryptocurrency trading many different exchanges off different coins. Sometimes the coins you hold are on different exchanges and seeing your holdings requires logging into several different sites. With Block Manager you can combine all all your holdings across all exchanges and compare it with live price data. Making Block Manager a useful tool for a cryptocurrency portfolio tracking.

How can I use Block Manager?

Block manager is completely free! Just log in and create an account! You don't even have to really hold any cryptocurrencies as you choose how much you bought and what price you bought at. Block Manager is a perfect tool for anyone who is trying to learn about crypto trading without any real losses or even experienced traders.
